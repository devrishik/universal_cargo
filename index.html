With more than two decades experience in global air freight services we have successfully serving different business centres globally. Sea Freight

Universal Cargo handle FCL (Full Container Load) sea shipments and facilitate to make a cargo to reach at stuffing point, arrange customs processing to stuff the cargo in containers and effect the shipment within minimum possible time. We also ship the cargo on LCL (Less Container Load) basis, with minimum freight and offer fastest transit time possible and do the consolidations of small shipments from different shippers/exporters to facilitate at the economical air/sea freight and arrange warehousing until the custom clearance of air /sea cargo, taking due care of special instruction, if any, and carry it to its entire length. Teamed up with some best project Cargo movers, having agents all over the world; we also provide a complete package service of Door to Door at very competitive price.
